MathIT HK

Online Platform for Maths Courses tailored for Hong Kong DSE Students

Project Overview

MathIT HK is an online platform designed to offer specialized maths courses for Hong Kong DSE students. Through a comprehensive curriculum, students can better prepare for their exams and achieve academic success.

Demo

Technology Stack

  • Front-end: ReactJS, Redux, Material-UI
  • Back-end: NodeJS, Express, MongoDB
  • Deployment: Docker, AWS
